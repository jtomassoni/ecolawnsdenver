## Image Specifications for All Images

### Technical Requirements:
- **Resolution:** Minimum 1920x1080 for hero images, 1200x800 for content images
- **Format:** JPG or WebP for web optimization
- **File Size:** Optimized for web (under 500KB when possible)
- **Aspect Ratios:** 
  - Hero: 16:9
  - Content: 16:9 or 4:3
  - Icons: 1:1
  - Banners: 3:1 or 4:1

### Style Guidelines:
- Professional photography style
- Bright, clear, inviting
- Green, healthy-looking lawns
- Clean, modern aesthetic
- Trustworthy and professional appearance
- Mobile-optimized (readable on small screens)
- Denver/Colorado context when appropriate

### Color Scheme:
- Primary: Green (#2E7D32, #1B5E20)
- Secondary: White, light backgrounds
- Accent: Natural greens, blues (sky)
- Avoid: Overly saturated or unrealistic colors
